# Code of Conduct

reliakit follows the **Rust Code of Conduct**. As a project in the Rust
ecosystem, we hold ourselves to the same standard the wider community uses.

The full text is at:

https://www.rust-lang.org/policies/code-of-conduct

## In short

Be friendly and patient, be welcoming, be considerate, and be respectful. Choose
your words with care, and when people disagree, try to understand why. Behavior
that is disrespectful, demeaning, or harassing is not tolerated.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any other official channel — and whenever someone is
representing the project in public.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
maintainer at **satyakwok88@gmail.com**. Reports are handled confidentially. The
maintainers will review every report, respond as needed, and respect the privacy
and security of the reporter.
